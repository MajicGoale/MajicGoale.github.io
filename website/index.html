The 50th day

Fantasy, Tower defence, strategy!

About the game

The 50th day is a tower defence game where the player starts with almost nothing, and as they progress through the days they send their villagers to collect materials to build new and powerful towers to hold back the forces of the demon lord.

Learn more

Story overview

Your village is under attack by the demon lord’s army! You’ve sent a messenger to the king but unfortunately it seems the demon lord has attacked many villages at once and so it will take the king 50 days before he is able to provide aid to your village. As the mayor it is your job to defend the village until the kings forces can arrive.

Learn more

Gameplay

Send your villagers out to collect resources and gold, and then use these materials to build awesome new towers to prepare your defences against the armies of evil creatures that are sent at you.
